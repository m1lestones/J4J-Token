## âš ï¸ Risk Considerations

### Dual-Chain Risks

1. **Liquidity Split:** Two pools = divided liquidity
2. **Price Divergence:** Prices may differ between chains
3. **Confusion:** Some users may buy wrong chain
4. **Double Work:** Managing two ecosystems

### Mitigations

1. **Clear Communication:** Always specify chain in posts
2. **Landing Page:** Chain selector with clear instructions
3. **Unified Branding:** Same visuals, same message
4. **Community Education:** FAQs and guides
